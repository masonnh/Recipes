Sweet Breakfast Sandwhich

Description

This is a super easy recipe to eat for breakfast or a snack when you want a sweet breakfast sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as NutellaÂ®)
  • 2 English muffins, split and toasted
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons marshmallow cream

Steps

  1. Spread 1 tablespoon chocolate-hazelnut spread on 1 half of each English muffin; arrange banana slices on top. Spread 1 tablespoon marshmallow cream on the other half. Press both halves together to create a sandwich.
